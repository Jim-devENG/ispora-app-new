# Ispora Monetization Strategy
*Documentation Date: April 3, 2026*

## 🎯 Executive Summary

**Current Status:** MVP - 100% Free (Months 0-6)  
**Long-term Model:** Freemium (Students) + Hybrid Mentor Compensation + Diversified Revenue  
**Philosophy:** Social Enterprise - Mission-driven but financially sustainable

**Key Revenue Streams:**
1. **Student Freemium** - Premium features for serious students ($5-7/month)
2. **Mentor Marketplace** - Optional paid mentorship (20% platform fee)
3. **Corporate Sponsorships** - Companies sponsor mentors ($500-5k/month)
4. **Talent Placement** - Job placement fees (Year 3+)
5. **Donations & Grants** - Community support + development grants

## 👨‍🏫 MENTOR COMPENSATION STRATEGY

### **🎯 Philosophy: Hybrid Model**

**Core Principle:** 
Mentors should have the **choice** between volunteering (giving back) and earning (valuing their time). Both options coexist on the platform.

**Why Hybrid:**
- ✅ **Respects mentor autonomy** - They choose how to participate
- ✅ **Increases mentor supply** - Attracts both volunteers AND busy professionals
- ✅ **Improves retention** - Paid mentors stick around longer (financial incentive)
- ✅ **Maintains mission** - Free mentorship still available for all students
- ✅ **Generates revenue** - Platform fee on paid sessions funds growth
- ✅ **Competitive positioning** - Can recruit top mentors from other platforms

---

### **📅 Mentor Compensation Rollout**

#### **Phase 1 (Months 0-12): 100% Volunteer**
- All mentors donate their time for free
- Focus on mission-driven diaspora who want to give back
- Build community, prove value, establish quality
- **Goal:** 50+ active volunteer mentors

#### **Phase 2 (Months 13-15): Beta Testing Paid Tier**
- Invite 10-20 experienced mentors to beta test paid option
- Set pricing guidelines ($15-75/session range)
- Test payment processing (Stripe Connect)
- Gather feedback from both paid mentors and their students
- Refine policies and user experience

#### **Phase 3 (Month 16+): Official Hybrid Launch**
- All mentors can choose: **Volunteer** or **Paid** (or both!)
- Platform takes 20% fee on paid sessions
- Free mentors still available for students
- Corporate sponsorship program launches

- Student pays **$50** for a session

**What the 20% Platform Fee Covers:**
- Payment processing (Stripe fees ~3%)
- Platform infrastructure (servers, storage)
- Customer support for payment issues
- Scheduling & calendar tools
- Video calling infrastructure
- Mentor profile hosting
- Marketing to attract students
- Trust & safety (dispute resolution)

**Competitive Comparison:**
- **Ispora:** 20% platform fee
- **MentorCruise:** 25-30% fee
- **ADPList:** 15% fee (but limited features)
- **Upwork:** 20% fee (10% for top earners)
- **Fiverr:** 20% fee

**Why 80/20 is Fair:**
- Mentor keeps majority of earnings
- Lower than most competitors
- Covers real platform costs
- Allows for platform sustainability

### **💳 Payment Processing for Mentors**

#### **How Mentors Get Paid:**

**Technology:** Stripe Connect (industry standard)

**Process:**
1. Student books paid session → Pays $50 via Stripe
2. Ispora holds funds in escrow
3. Session happens
4. After session completes:
   - Mentor gets $40 (80%)
   - Ispora keeps $10 (20%)
5. Funds transferred to mentor's bank account

**Payout Schedule:**
- **Option A:** Instant payout (available within 24 hours)
- **Option B:** Weekly batch (every Monday)
- **Option C:** Monthly (1st of each month)

**Payout Methods:**
- Bank transfer (ACH for US, SWIFT for international)
- Debit card (instant, small fee)
- PayPal (for international mentors)
- Mobile money (for Nigerian mentors)

**Tax Handling:**
- Mentors are independent contractors
- Ispora issues 1099 (US) or equivalent tax forms
- Mentors responsible for own taxes
- Annual earnings statement provided

### **🛡️ Protection & Quality Control**

#### **For Students:**

**Money-Back Guarantee:**
- If mentor doesn't show up → **Full refund**
- If session is < 15 minutes → **Full refund**
- If major quality issues → **Partial refund** (case-by-case)

**Rating System:**
- Students rate mentors after each session (1-5 stars)
- Written reviews (public)
- Mentors below 4.0 average → Reviewed by admin
- Mentors below 3.5 average → Removed from paid tier

**Escrow System:**
- Payment held until session completes
- Protects against mentor no-shows
- Automatic release after session

---

#### **For Mentors:**

**No-Show Protection:**
- Student doesn't show up within 15 minutes → Mentor gets paid
- Student cancels < 24 hours before → Mentor gets 50% fee
- Chronic student no-shows → Student account flagged

**Dispute Resolution:**
- Ispora mediates payment disputes
- Video call recordings available (if enabled) for evidence
- Fair review process before refunds

**Quality Standards:**
- Mentors must maintain 4.0+ star rating
- Response time: Reply to student requests within 48 hours
- Professional conduct policy
- Violations → Warning → Suspension → Removal

- Monitor average session prices

### **📞 Mentor FAQs**

**Q: Will I be pressured to charge for sessions?**
> A: Absolutely not. Volunteer mentorship will always be celebrated and supported. The paid tier is 100% optional for mentors who want to offer it.

**Q: Can I offer some free and some paid sessions?**
> A: Yes! Many mentors do a hybrid model - 2-3 free slots per month to give back, plus paid slots for additional availability.

**Q: What if a student can't afford my rate?**
> A: You can offer sliding scale, "pay what you can," or direct them to volunteer mentors. Some mentors reserve 20% of slots for scholarship students.

**Q: How do taxes work?**
> A: You're an independent contractor. Ispora provides annual earnings statements (1099 for US mentors). You're responsible for your own taxes. Consult a tax professional.

**Q: What if I want to raise my rates later?**
> A: You can update your rates anytime. Existing booked sessions honor the old rate, but new bookings use the new rate.

**Q: Can I mentor outside of Ispora for higher rates?**
> A: Yes, but we ask that students you meet on Ispora stay on the platform (our Terms of Service). This protects both of you with our dispute resolution and tools.

## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

### **Risk #1: Too Few Students Upgrade**

**Prevention:**
- Make free tier genuinely valuable (not crippled)
- Ensure Plus features have obvious value
- Price at psychological sweet spot ($5-7, not $15)
- Offer scholarships generously

**Backup Plan:**
- Focus on donations + sponsorships for revenue
- Add "supporter tier" ($2-3/month) with just badge + warm fuzzy feeling
- Consider "pay what you can" model

---

### **Risk #2: Payment Infrastructure Breaks**

**Prevention:**
- Use battle-tested services (Paystack, Stripe)
- Test thoroughly in sandbox
- Have fallback payment method (bank transfer)
- Clear error messages + support contact

**Backup Plan:**
- Manual payment processing (send invoice, confirm manually)
- Temporary "free Plus" for affected users
- Email alternative payment link

---

### **Risk #3: Users Feel Nickel-and-Dimed**

**Prevention:**
- Over-communicate that free tier stays valuable forever
- Frame Plus as "optional acceleration" not "required features"
- Be generous with scholarships
- Make pricing transparent (no hidden fees)

**Backup Plan:**
- Survey users on perceived value
- Adjust pricing/features based on feedback
- Consider bundling features differently

---

### **Risk #4: High Churn (Users Cancel Quickly)**

**Prevention:**
- Deliver value immediately (don't make them wait)
- Send engagement emails (feature tips, success stories)
- Track usage and reach out to inactive Plus users
- Exit survey when cancelling (learn why)

**Response:**
- Offer "pause subscription" instead of cancel
- Win-back campaign after 30 days ("We miss you")
- Discount to re-activate (50% off for 3 months)

- $10/month = sponsor 2 students to have Plus access
- Sponsor gets thank-you video from student quarterly
- **Pros:** Leverages diaspora giving, emotional connection

- **Pros:** Clear value ladder, easier to upsell

- Platform stays 100% free for students

- Ispora takes 20% commission
- **Pros:** No student payment friction, mentors earn
- **Cons:** Reduces volunteer mentor pool, changes mission
